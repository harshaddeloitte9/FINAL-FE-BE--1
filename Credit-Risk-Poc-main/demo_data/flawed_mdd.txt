Model: XGBoost Credit Default Classifier
Owner: Risk Analytics Team
Version: 1.0
Date: 2023-12-01

We trained an XGBoost model on internal loan data to predict default.
Standard preprocessing was applied. The model uses applicant and loan features.
Performance: ROC-AUC: 0.78, Gini: 0.56, KS Statistic: 0.48,
CV Mean AUC: 0.73, Accuracy: 0.79, Precision: 0.61,
Recall: 0.72, F1 Score: 0.65.
Model is suitable for credit decisioning purposes.
